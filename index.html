Tips for Agenda Review

  • Look for Zoning Change (ZC) requests from AG/SF to higher density
  • Check for Preliminary Plat and Final Plat items
  • Look for Specific Use Permits (SUP) related to residential
  • Review Comprehensive Plan Amendments for land use changes
  • Check Development Agreements and PD ordinances
  • Note any annexation requests for future development areas
  • Track impact fee discussions that affect lot costs
